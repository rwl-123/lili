Cavalier

The Cavalier King Charles Spaniel is a small, affectionate, and friendly breed known for its expressive eyes and silky, flowing coat. With a gentle and loving nature, Cavaliers are excellent companions, making them ideal pets for families, seniors, and individuals alike. They are known for their social personalities and are generally good with children, other dogs, and even strangers. Though they enjoy outdoor play, they are just as happy curling up on the couch for a cuddle. Their sweet and adaptable temperament, along with their beauty, makes them one of the most beloved toy breeds worldwide.
